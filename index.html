About Me

Hi, I'm John. I live in Vancouver and work as a web developer at PNI Digital Media. I've been in Vancouver for almost 2 years and I love it here. I used to work for Central 1 as a Software Specialist 2 and I'm originally from Ireland, where I worked as a Java Developer for 3 years. I'm much more interested in front-end development and design though, and I'm always looking to learn the latest tech and trends in the industry.

In my current position I make use of a lot of cool technologies like AngularJS, SASS, PostCSS, jQuery and plain ol' javaScript with grunt task runner and git. All on a .net platform. I tend to use WebStorm as my IDE of choice, with Visual Studio for any back end changes.

I enjoy hiking, walking and travelling, skiing and playing board games, the occasional social drink ;) and exploring this beautiful country.
